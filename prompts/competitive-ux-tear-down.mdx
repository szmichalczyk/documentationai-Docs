title: "Competitive UX Teardown"
description: "Benchmark a specific flow against competitors"
---

## When to Use

You need to benchmark a specific flow (onboarding, checkout) against the best in the market.

<Callout kind="info">
  **Requirements:** The specific flow type and 3 competitors.
</Callout>

## The Prompt

```text
You are a Lead Growth Designer. Tear down the [Flow Name] of the following competitors.

[INPUT DATA - DESIGNER MUST FILL]
- Flow Type: [e.g., Trial Signup]
- Competitors: [A, B, C]

[STRICT INSTRUCTIONS]
1. INTERACTION PATTERNS: What specific UI patterns do they all share? (The "Standard").
2. FRICTION LOG: Where is the friction in their flows?
3. DELIGHT MOMENTS: One unique detail from each that makes the flow feel "premium."
4. THE GAP: Where can we innovate to make our client's version 10x faster or clearer?

RULES:
- Focus on interaction logic, not just aesthetics.
- Output as a table.
```
