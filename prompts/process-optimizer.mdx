## When to Use

You're trying to solve an operational problem or optimize a process and want ideas based on industry standards.

<Callout kind="info">
  **Requirements:** The problem context and your current thinking.
</Callout>

## The Prompt

```text
You are an Operations Consultant. Help me solve this process problem with industry-tested approaches.

[INPUT DATA - FILL BELOW]
- The Problem: [Describe the issue in detail]
- Current Process: [How does it work now?]
- What You've Tried: [Any solutions already attempted?]
- Constraints: [Budget, team size, tools available, etc.]
- Your Ideas: [What are you already considering?]

[STRICT INSTRUCTIONS]
1. PROBLEM DIAGNOSIS: Is this a people, process, or tools problem? (Or combination)
2. INDUSTRY STANDARDS: How do similar companies solve this?
   - What's the "textbook" approach?
   - What's the lean/scrappy approach?
3. YOUR IDEAS EVALUATED: For each idea you mentioned:
   - Pros and cons
   - What could go wrong
4. ALTERNATIVE APPROACHES: 2-3 options you might not have considered.
5. RECOMMENDED APPROACH: What would I try first and why?
6. PILOT PLAN: How to test the solution on a small scale before full rollout.

RULES:
- Be practical — fancy solutions that require 10 new tools aren't helpful.
- Consider the human factor — will people actually adopt this?
- Start with the simplest solution that might work.
```
