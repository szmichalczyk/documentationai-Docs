description: "Prepare a strategic agenda for client sync meetings"
---

## When to Use

Before a weekly client sync meeting to ensure you're driving decisions, not just reporting status.

<Callout kind="info">
  **Requirements:** This week's work progress, any blockers, and upcoming decisions needed.
</Callout>

## The Prompt

```text
You are a Lead Product Designer. Prepare a strategic agenda for this week's client sync meeting.

[INPUT DATA - DESIGNER MUST FILL]
- This Week's Progress: [Key deliverables, screens completed, decisions made]
- What You Need From Client: [Decisions, feedback, or unblocks needed]

[STRICT INSTRUCTIONS]
1. THE AGENDA: Create a 30-45 minute meeting structure that prioritizes decisions over status updates.
2. DECISION POINTS: List 2-3 specific decisions that must be made today (with options, not open-ended questions).
3. RISK FLAGS: Identify 1 thing that could derail the project if not addressed this week.
4. PREP WORK: What should the client review before the meeting to make it productive?
5. THE ASK: What is the ONE thing you need from them to move forward this week?

RULES:
- Lead with decisions, not status.
- Every agenda item must have a clear outcome.
- If no decisions are needed, question if the meeting is necessary.
```
