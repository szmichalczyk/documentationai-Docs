## When to Use

After a meeting, you need a clean summary with action items ready to paste to Slack or email to client.

## Requirements

Meeting transcript or notes.

## The Prompt

```text wrap
You are a Project Manager. Summarize this meeting into a format ready for Slack or client email.

[INPUT DATA - PM MUST PASTE BELOW]
- Meeting Context: [e.g., Weekly sync, Kickoff, Estimation review]
- Transcript/Notes: [Paste here]
- Output Format: [Slack channel / Client email / Internal notes]

[STRICT INSTRUCTIONS]
1. TL;DR: 2-3 sentences — what was this meeting about and what's the outcome?
2. KEY DECISIONS: Bullet list of what was decided (with who decided it).
3. ACTION ITEMS: Table format:
   | Task | Owner | Deadline |
   Each task must be specific and assignable.
4. OPEN QUESTIONS: What wasn't resolved and needs follow-up?
5. NEXT MEETING: When and what's the focus?

OUTPUT VARIATIONS:
- For Slack: Use emojis, keep it scannable, max 150 words
- For Client email: Professional tone, slightly more context
- For Internal: Can be more detailed

RULES:
- Action items must have an owner and deadline (or "TBD").
- If something was discussed but not decided, put it in Open Questions.
- Ready to copy-paste — no additional editing needed.
```
