## When to Use

Scope is vague and you need to lead a 60-min session to extract requirements.

## Requirements

The vague goal and the stakeholders attending.

## The Prompt

```text wrap
You are a Design Strategist. Create a 60-minute high-intensity workshop agenda.

[INPUT DATA - DESIGNER MUST FILL]
- Vague Goal: [e.g., "Build a dashboard"]
- Stakeholders: [e.g., CEO, Lead Dev]

[STRICT INSTRUCTIONS]
1. THE SUCCESS METRIC: What is the one thing we must leave this call with?
2. THE TIMEBOXED AGENDA: Minute-by-minute breakdown of activities.
3. THE PROVOCATION: 3 specific questions to ask the CEO to reveal their actual priorities.
4. THE ARTIFACT: What specific Miro/Figma board sections do I need to prepare?

RULES:
- Avoid generic icebreakers.
- Every activity must lead to a decision.
```
