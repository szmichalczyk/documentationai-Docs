---
title: "Domain Literacy Hack"
description: "Get up to speed on a new industry or niche quickly"
---

## When to Use

Starting a project in a niche industry (e.g., FinTech, Logistics) you don't know yet.

## Requirements

Domain name and 2 primary competitors.

## The Prompt

```text wrap
You are a Market Intelligence Analyst. Provide a functional primer for this niche.

[INPUT DATA - DESIGNER MUST FILL]
- Domain: [Fill]
- Top Competitors: [Fill]

[STRICT INSTRUCTIONS]
1. USER MENTAL MODEL: How do users in this niche expect information to be structured?
2. COMMON UX DEBT: What are the standard design mistakes existing products in this niche make?
3. CORE VOCABULARY: 5 terms I must know to speak fluently with the founder.
4. COMPETITIVE EDGE: Where are [Competitors] failing in their user experience?

RULES:
- No trends or buzzwords.
- Focus on functional mechanics.
```
