## How to Use

1. **Find the right prompt** — Browse by category or use search
2. **Copy the prompt** — Each page has a copy button
3. **Fill in your context** — Replace the bracketed placeholders with your data
4. **Paste into Claude/ChatGPT** — Get structured, actionable output

## Who This Is For

Product designers, brand designers, project managers, no-code developers, and anyone at a digital agency who:
- Works with multiple clients on different projects
- Needs to move fast without sacrificing quality
- Wants consistent, professional communication
- Values structured thinking over ad-hoc prompting
